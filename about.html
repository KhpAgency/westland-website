About Us

Westland Established in 2020 by Mr. Franck Yves Lançon, Westland Trading LLC is a dynamic force in the food trading and poultry production sector, headquartered in Dubai, UAE, with a rich legacy of excellence and a relentless pursuit of innovation. Westland has rapidly become a trusted name in the local and international poultry industry. Westland manages a 200,000 m² chicken farm located in the Al Ain region of the Abu Dhabi emirate, operating under the name "Al Ajban Oasis Poultry Farm." This facility houses six boilers and is supported by a high-capacity chicken slaughterhouse that caters to both internal operations and external services.

mission

We are proud to contribute to one of the most vital sectors in UAE's food security. Our unwavering dedication ensures the delivery of premium-quality chicken products, aiming to establish ourselves as the leading supplier of top-tier poultry in the Gulf region.

vision

Our vision is to lead the way in nourishing future generations by locally producing the highest quality chicken. Through this commitment, we aim to provide Gulf households with a sustainable and nutritious source of protein, ensuring both health and environmental responsibility.
