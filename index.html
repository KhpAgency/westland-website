Alajban oasis poltry has two exceptional lines of chicken which is ( Albarari and Alajban). Albarari and alajban represents premium quality. We pride ourselves on providing premium poultry products, raised sustainably and in accordance with Islamic guidelines, from whole chickens to convenient cuts, we're dedicated to bringing you the best-tasting chicken, straight from our farm to your table.

Albarari chicken
project

Albarari chicken is a high-quality chicken. Our farm is dedicated to providing you with delicious poultry products that are produced sustainably and with care. We believe in simplicity and transparency, that's why we feed our chickens 100% natural feed and ensure that our slaughtering process follows Islamic Shariah guidelines. Whether you're looking for whole chickens or pre-cut pieces, we've got you covered. Our goal is to bring you the best-tasting chicken, straight from our farm to your table.

ALBARARI NATURAL
project

Albarari natural, It's a natural chicken that doesn't receive any type of antibiotics, free antibiotic. It's raised without any serums or medications, and its diet is completely natural 100%, slaughtered according to the islamic shariah consume within 3 days from production date that keep chilled at 0 c to +5c.
